ABOUT US

  • Serangreen Holdings (PVT) Ltd was a conducting business under diversified range such as Tea Plantation, Value Added Food & Beverages and Green Energy sectors.

  • Serangreen Holdings (PVT) Ltd established in 2021 under Company Act of Sri Lanka under the registration number of PV-00257597. The company mainly focusing on manufacturing organic mushroom sausage and organic value add products and innovative food solution to local and international consumers

  • Serangreen is currently located in Galle district near to Owilana, Sri Lanka. The Company produced quality natural food products, mainly using the quality organic mushrooms as main raw material.

  • Also company already maintain an organic mushroom farm using brand name of "Serangreen Mushrooms". Currently Serangreen product manufacture Mushroom Based Sausages, Organic Mushrooms, Soup Mixture Powder and Mushroom Veggie Balls.

  • Serangreen is certified by the ISO 22000 - 2018 international standards.
