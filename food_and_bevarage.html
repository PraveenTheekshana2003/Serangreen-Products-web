Frequently Asked Questions

➤ What makes your products unique?

Our products are made with fresh, organic ingredients sourced from trusted local farms. We prioritize sustainability and quality in every item we offer.


➤ Are your veggie products vegan and gluten-free?

Yes, most of our veggie products are vegan and gluten-free. We clearly label all our products to ensure you can make informed choices based on your dietary needs.


➤ Do your sausage products contain any artificial additives or preservatives?

No, our sausages are free from artificial additives and preservatives. We focus on creating a natural and healthy product without compromising on taste.
